Char::Transliterate::Kana

Hiragana/Katanaka convertions

SYNOPSIS

  use Char::Transliterate::Kana;
  
  $s = 'ねむいデス';
  katakana_to_hiragana $s;
  warn $s; # ねむいです
  hiragana_to_katakana $s;
  warn $s; # ネムイデス

DESCRIPTION

The Char::Transliterate::Kana module defines two functions to convert hiragana and katakana characters in text.

FUNCTIONS

By default, these functions are exported:

hiragana_to_katakana $string

Destructively replace hiragana letters in the specified string into katakana.

=itemkatakana_to_hiragana $string

Destructively replace katakana letters in the specified string into hiragana.

LIMITATIONS

Halfwidth katakana letters are intentionally not supported. They should be first converted to fullwidth letters by Char::Normalize::FullwidthHalfwidth.

Kana digraphs and additional small katakana letters are not converted, as their corresponding Hiragana or Katakana characters are not included in Unicode. Likewise, circled, squared, or other decorated kana characters are not converted.
